The stack
Honest limitations
  • No ground-truth speeds — CIS is a relative index, validated by a ±25% weight-sensitivity sweep, not against measured delay.
  • Timestamp = enforcement/sync time, not the violation instant — time used as a relative weight only.
  • The rupee figure is a transparent planning estimate; durable results are the concentration & ROI.
  • We only see where police ticketed — the rejection model and CV feed help correct this bias.
